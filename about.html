You might not remember, but a few years ago there was that movie you really wanted to see. The commercial looked amazing and you really meant to call your friends. But you got busy, didn't have a date, and you were running low on cash. Before you knew it the theater had moved on to the next blockbuster and you had missed your chance.

Fast-forward to now and you still don't have a date (for reasons we won't get into), so you're at home on Friday night scrolling through Netflix. You see a title and it kind of rings a bell; it piques your interest. You read the description and it's deja-vu! This is the film you wanted to see!

You can make up for lost time, but should you waste a couple hours on a movie that's past it's prime in a social context? Plus, there's a rumor that new Netflix original series is really good, but that might lend itself to a binge...

Simple solution!! Just hit up Sloth Review to see if it's still worth watching! Sloth Review has the best unbiased opinions and thoughtfully written reviews of movies that might not be the current hot ticket.
